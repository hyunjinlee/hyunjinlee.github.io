YOU SO, I-DEP
MOTION GRAPHIC
2012 CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM
This work is music video used song of "You So, I-dep" in CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM with HongIk Art University. It's non-narrative video and I expressed the concept of deconstruction because the music feeling is flicking and fitting jazz rhythm. The story is that one dot becomes an egg and graphics inside an egg are falling and deconstructing with body parts. I cared using live action technique and 3d motion graphics. Also, I tried to make surrealism layout.
The poster created by Lila and Edwin.
Students from Hong-Ik Uni. in Korea participate in a CalArts course titled 'Techniques and Design in Motion Graphics.'- Photo: Scott Groller
