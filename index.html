YOU SO, I-DEP
MOTION GRAPHIC
CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM
This work is music video used song of "You So, I-dep" in CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM with HongIk Art University. It's non-narrative video and I expressed the concept of deconstruction because the music feeling is flicking and fitting jazz rhythm. The story is that one dot becomes an egg and graphics inside the egg, they are falling and deconstructing with body parts. I cared using live action technique and 3d motion graphics. Also, I tried to make surrealism layout.

"FAIR'S FAIR CAMPAIGN" IN GRADUATION WORK, 2011
There are 4 objects on the leaf table. Each objects mean each process of trade. First is tree, second is plane, third is building and fourth is cup. If user put puzzle on the objects, the movie shows on the table.
Each puzzle has fair trade mark and free trade mark. If user wants see fair trade process, he put the fair trade mark puzzle on the object. They can see fair and free trade at the same time.
