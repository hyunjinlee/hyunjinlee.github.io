This work is music video used song of "You So, I-dep" in CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM with HongIk Art University. Also, I tried to make surrealism layout.

LET’S START FAIR TRADE TOGETHER
The work "Fair’s fair" is a Motion graphic Installation includes one’s tiny consuming habit is to be a good consumption from everyone with our best wishes. This work shows an importance of Free Trade by comparing with Fair Trade progressed around the world now and the normal Free Trade. There are four stages of objects that correspond to Fair trade and Free trade on the table. You can watch a story starting from a "Tea" village of Kenya bout Fair trade and Free trade when you put a puzzle into the object.
"FAIR'S FAIR CAMPAIGN" IN GRADUATION WORK, 2011
There are 4 objects on the leaf table. Each objects mean each process of trade. First is tree, second is plane, third is building and fourth is cup. If user put puzzle on the objects, the movie shows on the table.
Each puzzle has fair trade mark and free trade mark. If user wants see fair trade process, he put the fair trade mark puzzle on the object. They can see fair and free trade at the same time.
