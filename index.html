YOU SO, I-DEP
MOTION GRAPHIC
2012 CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM
This work is music video used song of "You So, I-dep" in CALARTS WINTER PROGRAM with HongIk Art University. It's non-narrative video and I expressed the concept of deconstruction because the music feeling is flicking and fitting jazz rhythm. The story is that one dot becomes an egg and graphics inside the egg, they are falling and deconstructing with body parts. I cared using live action technique and 3d motion graphics. Also, I tried to make surrealism layout.
THIS POSTER CREATED BY LILA AND EDWIN.
- JANUARY 27, 2012 - STUDENTS FROM HONG-Ik UNIVERSITY PARTICIPATE IN THE CALARTS COURSE TITLED 'TECHNIQUES AND DESIGNING IN MOTION GRAPHICS.'- PHOTO: SCOTT GROLLER
CAMPAIGN IN 2011
